    Fitts Law

    OshKosh B'gosh

  • https://www.oshkosh.com/

    • What I like about Fitts Law is the application to the user experience and the "ease of use" to get to a targeted area or information of the webpage. In the case of OshkoshB'gos I see Fitts Law exemplifided in the use of buttons with each of the categories of the clothes sizes they have. I liked the part on where Fitts Law states that the longer the distance the harded is to make a desition. in this case you dont have to spend extra time searching among all their inventory to find a size. The faster you get there, the higer the chances of making a purchase.
